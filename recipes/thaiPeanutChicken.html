Savory Thai Peanut Chicken & Veggie Bowl

Description

This savory and creamy Thai Peanut Chicken and Veggie Bowl is the perfect meal for busy days. With tender chicken, fresh veggies, and a rich, flavorful Thai peanut sauce, it comes together in no more than 10 minutes, making it a quick and delicious option for any time of day. Whether you're craving something comforting or looking to try a new twist on a classic, this bowl is sure to satisfy your taste buds!

Ingredients

  • Rotisserie chicken (shredded)
  • Fresh shredded carrots (about 2 cups)
  • Red onion (add to taste)
  • Frozen bag of edamame
  • 2 bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 zuchini squash
  • 1 bottle of peanut sauce (i like House of Thang Bangkok Peanut Dipping Sauce)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Line the bottom of a large oven-safe tray with parchment paper.
  3. Add the shredded rotisserie chicken.
  4. Wash and chop the red onion, bell peppers, and zucchini into uniform pieces, then add them in.
  5. Heat the frozen edamame according to the package instructions and add it to the cooking tray.
  6. Pour in the entire bottle of peanut sauce.
  7. Mix everything together until evenly coated with the sauce.
  8. Cook in the oven for about 25 minutes, or until heated through.

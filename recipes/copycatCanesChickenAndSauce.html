Copycat Canes Chicken and Sauce

Description

This quick and easy recipe copies Rasing Canes famous crispy chicken tenders and creamy sauce. It takes only 15 minutes to make. It's great meal to make when you have no time for cooking.

Ingredients

  • Real Good chicken strips
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 tsp worcesterchire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken strips in an airfryer, follow the packeged directions.
  2. In a container, add your mayo, ketchup, worcesterchire sauce, black pepper, garlic powder, and salt.
